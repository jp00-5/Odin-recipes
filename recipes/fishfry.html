Fish Fry

Description

A fish fry is a meal containing battered or breaded fried fish.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Vinegar and Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Oil
    •

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cut and clean the fish pieces
  2. Step 2: You can add all the ingredients into a spice grinder and grind to a paste.
  3. Step 3: Using your fingers, generously slather the marinade all over the fish pieces
  4. Step 4: Place in a bowl or plate, cover and set aside for 30 minutes.
  5. Step 5: When ready to fry the fish, heat oil in a pan until it almost begins to smoke. Once the oil is hot enough, gently place the fish pieces into the oil
  6. Step 6: Once the first side of the fish is a dark golden brown, gently flip over and keep covered until the other side is cooked
  • Step 7: Serve fish fry hot with sliced onions and lime wedges
