Barbeque Chicken

Description

Barbecue chicken consists of chicken parts or entire chickens that are barbecued, grilled or smoked. Barbecued chicken is one of the world's most popular barbecue dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into halves
  • Â¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps to cook

  1. Step 1: Cut 1/2-inch deep slashes in the skin-side of each chicken half: two cuts in each breast, two in each thigh, and one in each leg. Remove wing tips
  2. Step 2: Whisk vinegar, 2 tablespoons barbecue sauce, and garlic together in a large bowl. Place chicken in the bowl and turn to coat. Refrigirate 1 hour
  3. Step 3: Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate
  4. Step 4: >Remove chicken from the bowl and pat dry with paper towels. lace chicken halves, skin-side up, on a plate and season with salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder, and cayenne pepper.
  5. Step 5: Cook chicken, skin-side down, on the preheated grill until grill marks appear, 3 to 4 minutes. Turn chicken over, close the grill lid, and cook.
